Data sources

The datasets in this repository were compiled from publicly available sources,
including official reports, operational bulletins, and media coverage.

Primary sources include:

- ARSO (Slovenian Environment Agency) reports and weather information
- ELES transmission system operator reports and operational information
- Daily reports of the Administration for Civil Protection and Disaster Relief
- Publicly available news media articles

The datasets included in this repository contain derived information
(event extraction, classification, and aggregation) based on these sources.

Original source documents remain subject to their respective copyright
and usage policies.
